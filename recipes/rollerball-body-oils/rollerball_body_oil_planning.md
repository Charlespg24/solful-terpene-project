## Transdermal Science Summary

### Why Terpenes Work Through Skin

Terpenes are exceptionally skin-permeable due to their lipophilic nature (logP 2.7–4.8). They dissolve into the stratum corneum lipid bilayer and reach subcutaneous circulation. Key evidence: Jäger et al. demonstrated peak plasma linalool at **19 minutes** after skin massage, with 13.76 min half-life in serum.

Terpenes are also used as **penetration enhancers** in pharmaceutical formulations — they increase skin permeability for co-applied compounds.

### Terpene-by-Terpene Transdermal Profile

| Terpene | Key Oils | Transdermal Effect | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| β-Caryophyllene | Black pepper | CB2 agonism at peripheral tissue; localized anti-inflammatory | Sesquiterpene — more stable in carrier, works locally at application site |
| Myrcene | Lemongrass | GABA-A potentiation; muscle relaxant (dose-dependent) | High adipose affinity; accumulates subcutaneously and releases slowly |
| Linalool | Lavender | GABA-A anxiolytic; peak plasma 19 min from skin | 2× longer half-life in females (subcutaneous fat reservoir) |
| Limonene | Lemon, sweet orange | 5-HT1A serotonin modulation; mood elevation | Low boiling point = strong proximity inhalation volatilization off warm skin |
| α-Pinene | Pine | Acetylcholinesterase inhibition; alertness | **Third-phase t½ = 695 min (~11.6 hrs)** — extreme adipose affinity, slow sustained release |
| α-Bisabolol | German chamomile | Direct dermal anti-inflammatory; penetration enhancer for other terpenes | Adenosine receptor anxiolytic; purpose-built for skin delivery |

### Delivery Pathway Comparison

| Pathway | Onset | Duration | Primary Mechanism |
|---|---|---|---|
| Diffuser (inhalation) | Seconds–minutes | 30–60 min | Olfactory-limbic priming |
| Nasal inhaler | Seconds | 15–30 min | Concentrated receptor loading |
| **Rollerball (transdermal)** | **15–20 min** | **2–6+ hours** | **Systemic depot + proximity inhalation** |

The rollerball creates a **terpene time-release system** that maintains receptor engagement long after the diffuser phase ends.

## The Rollerball's Role in the TPL Protocol

### Full Ritual — Three Acts

**Step 1 — Set the space**
Start the diffuser with the strain-matched essential oil blend. 15–30 min pre-loading begins. Olfactory-limbic pathway primes A2a-CB1 heteromers.

**Step 2 — Prepare yourself**
Apply the rollerball to pulse points. This is the intentional transition moment — a physical behavioral cue that signals state change. Trigeminal and transdermal pathways activate.

**Step 3 — Consume**
The diffuser primed the room. The oil is on your body. You inhale cannabis into an already terpene-saturated receptor environment. The rollerball continues delivering terpenes transdermally through the entire session.

### Why This Extends the Protocol

The existing TPL protocol front-loads terpene exposure before consumption. The rollerball extends it through the experience — body heat becomes a sustained-release diffuser worn on the skin, volatilizing terpenes at proximity inhalation distance for the full duration.

## Application Sites

### Primary — Pulse Points (heat + volatilization)
- **Inner wrists** — proximity inhalation every time hands come to face
- **Inside of elbows** — large surface area, strong pulse, sustained warmth
- **Base of throat / décolletage** — closest pulse point to nose and mouth; best proximity inhalation site
- **Behind the ears** — warm, close to olfactory system, thinner skin = faster absorption

### Secondary — Effect-Specific
- **Temples** — for creative/uplifting strains (Pink Rider, Carambola); terpenes volatilize right next to the olfactory system
- **Chest / sternum** — most mechanistically sound for TPL; every inhale pulls volatilized terpenes directly into airway
- **Sore muscles** — for recovery strains (Mule Fuel); BCP CB2 activity works locally at the application site

### Application Order (Ritual Sequence)
**Chest → throat → behind ears → wrists**
Moves from most pharmacologically significant to most convenient. Takes 30 seconds.

**Avoid:** Areas immediately covered by tight clothing — traps volatiles in fabric instead of airways.

### Assembly Steps
1. Add essential oil drops into empty rollerball bottle first
2. Pour in argan oil
3. Top with FCO — leave 3–4mm from top for roller mechanism
4. Press roller ball firmly into cap until it clicks
5. Cap and roll gently between palms for 30 seconds — do not shake

### Evaluation Protocol
**Immediately** — terpinolene should read bright, slightly piney-citrus. Not medicinal.
**5 min** — limonene comes forward as terpinolene volatilizes off.
**15–20 min** — dry-down. BCP from black pepper anchors as warm, slightly spicy base.

**Iteration flag:** If tea tree reads medicinal, drop to 2 drops and bring basil up to 3.

- **Dispensary pitch:** Rollerball held up alongside flower jar — "matched to the exact terpene profile of this strain, apply before you consume."
