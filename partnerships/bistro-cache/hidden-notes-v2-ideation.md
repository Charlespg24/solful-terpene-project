# Hidden Notes v2 — Multi-Service Terpene Experience

## The Evolution

v1 was a 5-course dinner with terpene education and strain *references* on tasting cards. Beautiful, but passive — guests learn about terpenes through food and go to Solful later.

v2 makes cannabis an active part of the experience. Guests don't just learn about terpenes — they *consume* them, in both food and flower, across multiple services throughout 4/20. Solful isn't a referral destination anymore. It's the co-host, the product source, and a physical stop on the journey.

---

Each service has a curated **"Hidden Notes Basket"** that guests pre-order from Solful.

**What's Inside:**
- 2–3 pre-rolls OR single-serve vape carts (strain-matched to the service)

**Pricing Tiers:**

**Revenue:**

## The Two-Location Flow

This is what makes Hidden Notes unique — it's not just a dinner, it's a **journey between two locations**.

**Each service follows the same rhythm:**

```
SOLFUL (start)          →  CACHÉ (dining)           →  SOLFUL (optional return)
Pick up basket             Food + cocktails + oils      Guided tasting
Terpene briefing           Cannabis breaks between       Meet the Farmer connection
The 90-second walk         courses                      Take basket home
```

**Why this works:**
- Caché stays fully legal — no cannabis consumed inside
- Solful is the portal, the product source, and the bookend
- The walk itself becomes part of the experience (1.5 blocks, Inner Sunset)
- Guests experience Solful's space, staff, and expertise firsthand

## Legal Framework: AB 1775

California signed AB 1775 (effective January 1, 2025) — the "cannabis café" bill.

**What it allows:**
- Cannabis retailers with consumption endorsements can serve non-cannabis food and beverages
- Live music, performances, and ticketed events are permitted
- Local jurisdictions must opt in

**What this means for Hidden Notes:**
- Caché CANNOT permit cannabis consumption (restaurant, not cannabis retailer)
- Solful COULD apply for an on-site consumption endorsement (already a retailer)
- If Solful gets the endorsement, the pre-dinner terpene briefing could include on-site consumption at Solful before walking to Caché
- Long-term: Solful could become a cannabis café hosting its own food events

**For 4/20 2026 (practical approach):**
- Cannabis consumption happens outside Caché (SF is permissive)
- Edibles/tinctures from the basket are consumed by guests at their discretion
- The Solful basket is the product — how guests consume is their choice
- No consumption happens *inside* Caché
