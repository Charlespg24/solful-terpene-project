## Farm Context Notes

### Moon Gazer Farms (Josh & Sandra Khan, Redwood Valley, Mendocino)
- **Philosophy:** Biodynamic, seed-sown (not clones), sun-grown, full-season outdoor
- **Soil:** Hugelkulture — fallen trees + native soil + animal manure + mulch. Closed-loop system.
- **Certifications:** Demeter Biodynamic, Dragonfly Earth Medicine Pure
- **Key detail:** "Each seed is a little bit different which creates more variation. We love the diversity!"
- **Chickens:** Eat snails → calcium in droppings → back into soil
- **Josh quote:** "I don't think of myself as a grower — rather, a farmer."
- **Press:** Won 2016 Emerald Cup "Regenerative Farm Award"
- **Setting texture:** Redwood Valley, horse ranches, small homesteads, idyllic. Learned biodynamics at Frey Ranch (first biodynamic winery in the nation).
- **Used in:** Moonlight v2, Natty Bumppo, Black Lime Chem
