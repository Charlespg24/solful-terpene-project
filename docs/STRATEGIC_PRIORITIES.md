## Product Line Strategy

**The product is not just rollerballs.** The full Solful product portfolio:

| Tier | Product | Format | Status | Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Essential oil blends** (strain-matched, for diffuser) | 5–10ml amber dropper bottle | 24 blends formulated in strain JSONs, not extracted to retail packaging | **The core product — direct expression of the pre-loading protocol** |
| **2** | **Rollerball body oils** (strain-matched, personal format) | 10ml glass roller | Pink Rider recipe documented; 4 more launch SKUs planned | Portable, extends protocol through transdermal delivery |
| **3** | **Soy wax candles** (strain-matched) | 6–8oz jar, cotton wick | 24 candle recipes already in strain JSONs (Layer 8 — scent notes, burn time) | Passive ambient diffusion, gift-friendly retail, strong margins |
| **4** | **Shower steamers** (terpene-infused) | Pressed tablet | Not started — formulation TBD | High-margin passive-delivery format, bath/wellness crossover |
| **5** | **Pre-made juice/tea blends** (strain-matched beverages) | — | 120 beverage recipes written; no co-packer engagement | Long-term; requires commercial kitchen or co-packer |

**Solful launch pitch = tiers 1 + 2 together (with Tier 3 candles as a stretch add-on if time permits)**, not rollerballs alone. The EO blend is the pharmacological primary; the rollerball is the portable extension; the candle is the ambient/gift format. Pitching the tiered portfolio gives Solful a full protocol to sell, not a single SKU.
