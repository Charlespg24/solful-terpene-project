## Strain 01: Mike's Bomba

**Farm:** Glentucky Family Farm · Sonoma
**Energy:** LOW
**Effects:** Relaxed, Calm, Alert
**Intent:** Grounded calm with clear mental edges
**Aroma:** Fuel, Lemon Cream, Forest Floor
**Cannabinoids:** THC: 28.76% | CBD: 0.09%
**Total Terpenes:** 1.38%

### Terpene Profile

| Terpene | % | Ratio |
|---------|---|-------|
| β-Caryophyllene | 0.47 | 45% |
| Limonene | 0.32 | 31% |
| α-Humulene | 0.18 | 17% |
| Linalool | 0.07 | 7% |

### Essential Oil Blend (40 drops / 2ml)

Black Pepper (20), Lemon (10), Sage (8), Lavender (2)

### Juice Recipe: Grounded Forest Calm

**Ingredients:** 2.5 oz pink grapefruit juice, 1 oz lemon juice, 0.75 oz fresh ginger juice, 1 fresh rosemary sprig, 0.25 oz honey, pinch sage powder, pinch ground black pepper, 2 drops lavender flavor extract, 2 oz sparkling water

**Method:**
1. Press 1 oz fresh ginger root through a fine grater or juicer
2. Steep rosemary sprig in 1 oz warm water for 3 min, strain and cool
3. Combine grapefruit juice, lemon juice, and ginger juice
4. Add rosemary water, honey, sage powder, black pepper, and lavender flavor drops
5. Pour over ice in a rocks glass, top with sparkling water down the side
6. Garnish with rosemary sprig

**Terpene Map:** β-Caryophyllene (black pepper, ginger, rosemary) · Limonene (grapefruit, lemon) · α-Humulene (sage, ginger) · Linalool (lavender flavor)

### Essential Oil Blend (40 drops / 2ml)

Black Pepper (14), Lemon (10), Sage (6), Lemongrass (6), Geranium (4)

### Juice Recipe: Frontier Ease

**Ingredients:** 2.5 oz tart cherry juice, 0.5 oz lemon juice, 0.5 oz cardamom-vanilla syrup, 0.5 oz fresh ginger juice, pinch ground black pepper, 1 fresh rosemary sprig, 2 oz cold mineral water

**Method:**

| α-Pinene | 0.39 | 15% |
| β-Caryophyllene | 0.27 | 11% |
| β-Ocimene | 0.19 | 7% |

### Essential Oil Blend (40 drops / 2ml)

Lemongrass (16), Pine (8), Black Pepper (6), Basil (6), Lemon (4)

### Juice Recipe: Weighted Lime Bliss

**Ingredients:** 2 oz lime juice, 1.5 oz green apple juice (fresh pressed), 1 fresh rosemary sprig, 4 fresh Prospera basil leaves, 0.25 oz cinnamon-honey syrup, pinch ground cinnamon, 1 oz cold mineral water

**Method:**
1. Make cinnamon-honey syrup: simmer 1 cinnamon stick in 2 oz water + 1 oz honey for 5 min, strain and cool
2. Press 1 green apple, blend and strain to yield 1.5 oz
3. Steep rosemary sprig in 1 oz warm water for 2 min, strain
4. Gently muddle basil leaves for 2 seconds
5. Combine lime juice, green apple juice, rosemary water, cinnamon-honey syrup, and cinnamon, stir with ice 8 seconds
6. Strain into rocks glass over large ice, add mineral water, garnish with lime wheel and rosemary

**Terpene Map:** Myrcene (lime, rosemary, basil) · α-Pinene (rosemary, basil) · β-Caryophyllene (cinnamon, basil) · β-Ocimene (basil)

**Substitution Notes:** Green apple replaces rhubarb for tartness. If rhubarb available, juice 1 cup chopped rhubarb and use 1.5 oz in place of apple.

## Batch Syrups (make once, use across recipes)

### Cardamom-Honey Syrup
Crack 3-4 cardamom pods in 2 oz warm water + 1 tbsp honey. Steep 5 min, strain. Keeps 2 weeks refrigerated.
**Used in:** Carambola, Rasta Governmint

### Cardamom-Vanilla Syrup
Crack 5 cardamom pods in 4 oz water + 2 oz honey + 0.5 tsp vanilla extract. Simmer 5 min, strain. Keeps 2 weeks refrigerated.
**Used in:** Natty Bumppo

### Cinnamon-Honey Syrup
Simmer 1 cinnamon stick in 2 oz water + 1 oz honey for 5 min. Strain and cool. Keeps 2 weeks refrigerated.
**Used in:** Black Lime Chem, Blueberry Muffin, Strawberry Biscotti
